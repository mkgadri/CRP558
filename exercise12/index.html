Information Included in the Style Template

The data included in the style Template shows the location of hospital in Washington State, obtained from Washington Geospatial Open Data. Changes made to the hospital points includes a change in Color to Green, Stroke Width to 2 and Radius to 8. Also, changes made to the road layer obtained from Mapbox Streets v8 Source by filtering the feature type to primary,secondary, tertiary, trucks and motoways include changing the Opacity to 0.8, Gap Width to 2, Width of road itself to 1 and Join to Round.
