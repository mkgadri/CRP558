BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Mining is an inherently destructive industry, and the mining effects of even a single operation can have a severe impact on the environment and the wildlife that lives nearby. Although there are some regulations in place that are intended to minimize the damage, they are not enough to allow mining and wildlife to exist in harmony, especially in cases where the regulations are difficult to enforce.The mining industry has the potential to disrupt ecosystems and wipe out wildlife populations in several different ways.Mining is not going to stop anytime soon, but itâ€™s possible to lessen their negative impact on the environment and wildlife.Responsible mining will not only save the environment and wildlife, but it can also ensure the safety of the people working in the mine and living in nearby areas. Thus, the purpose of this project is to create an interactive map and that shows the spatial location of mineral resources and areas of concentration of various mineral types, and how their exploration could affect available nature reserves.
